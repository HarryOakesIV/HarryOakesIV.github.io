About Me

I started my career behind the camera as a videographer and camera operator, learning production from the ground up. But the more I worked, the more I realized my passion wasn't just capturing imagesâ€”it was shaping stories from concept to screen.

That realization led me to pursue an M.A. in Producing at American University, where I focused on development, pitching, and fundraising. Now, as a Senior Filmmaker at the Kennedy Center, I combine both worlds: leading productions for major networks while staying hands-on with the craft.

I've had the privilege of working on high-profile broadcasts like the Kennedy Center Honors and Mark Twain Prize, collaborating with incredible artists and reaching millions of viewers. My goal is to continue growing as a producer, with a long-term focus on development and distribution in the film and television industry.
