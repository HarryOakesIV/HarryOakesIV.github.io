Hi, I'm Harry

Producer | Filmmaker | Kennedy Center

I'm a producer and filmmaker at the Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, where I create content for major broadcast networks and streaming platforms. With an M.A. in Producing from American University and hands-on experience across the entire production pipeline, I bring both creative vision and technical execution to every project. My work has featured artists like Conan O'Brien, Francis Ford Coppola, and Kevin Hart, reaching audiences on Netflix, CBS, and PBS.

About Me

I started my career behind the camera as a videographer and camera operator, learning production from the ground up. But the more I worked, the more I realized my passion wasn't just capturing images—it was shaping stories from concept to screen.

That realization led me to pursue an M.A. in Producing at American University, where I focused on development, pitching, and fundraising. Now, as a Senior Filmmaker at the Kennedy Center, I combine both worlds: leading productions for major networks while staying hands-on with the craft.

I've had the privilege of working on high-profile broadcasts like the Kennedy Center Honors and Mark Twain Prize, collaborating with incredible artists and reaching millions of viewers. My goal is to continue growing as a producer, with a long-term focus on development and distribution in the film and television industry.
